About Our Web App's Sections

Our Web App Consists Of 6 Sections Namely:-

1.Home

2.Updates

3.Classes

4.Library

Instructions:

* In The Home Section You Will Get About Information Of Our Web App And Instructions To Use Our Web App.

* In The Updates Section You Will Get All The Recent Announcements.

* In The Classes Section You Will Get The Live Classes' Links And Also A Folder Consisting Of Previous Classes In Recorded Form Which You Can Watch Anytime.

* In The Library Section You Will Get All Of Your Required Study Materials Which Includes Textbooks,Reference Books,Hand Books,Notes etc.

* In The Assignments Section You Will Get All Your Due And Submitted Assignments.

* In The Results Section You Will Get Your Results,Marksheet And Admit Card.

So, Enjoy Using Our Web App For Your Study Requirements!
